# Code of Conduct

FlexAID∆S is a project built for serious science and real people, including those who use drugs and live with mental illness. The community standards reflect that.

## 1. Our Pledge

We commit to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or lived experience.

## 2. Expected Behavior

- Focus on technical and scientific arguments, not personal attacks.
- Use direct, honest language without dehumanizing anyone.
- Respect cross-disciplinary expertise (computing, pharmacology, psychiatry, lived experience).
- Assume good faith but do not ignore harm.

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or targeted insults.
- Stigmatizing language about people who use drugs or people with mental health conditions.
- Trolling, flaming, or deliberate derailment of discussions.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## 4. Reporting

Reports can be made through GitHub or directly to the maintainer. Maintainers will review and act at their discretion, which may include warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans from contribution.

## 5. Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, tuned for the mission and values of FlexAID∆S.
